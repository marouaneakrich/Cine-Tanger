The Vision

Seamless Discovery

Creating a centralized 3D hub where movie-goers can explore all local screenings without friction.

Visual Mastery

Redefining how users interact with seat selections and ticket management.

Core Features

  • Interactive Seat Selection: Real-time mapping of theater halls with direct seat reservation.
  • Dynamic Movie Hub: Immersive browsing with integrated ratings and synopses.
  • Smart QR Wallet: Secure local storage for tickets with offline accessibility.
  • Contextual Filtering: Quick discovery by session time, room name, or movie genre.
